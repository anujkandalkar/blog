About

Dr. Geetika Narang is an Associate Professor and Head of the Computer Department at Trinity College of Engineering and Research, Pune. With over 17 years of experience in teaching both undergraduate and postgraduate courses, she has been serving as the Head of the Department for the past 5 years. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and specializes in Networking and Data Mining.

A passionate lecturer and researcher, Dr. Narang has published over 35 papers in national and international journals and conferences, with 15 of them indexed in Scopus. She is also an approved Co-Guide at Amity University, Jaipur and JJTU. Her academic contributions reflect a deep commitment to research, mentorship, and advancing the field of computer science.
